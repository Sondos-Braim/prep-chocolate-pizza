Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON 15 DEC 2013/DESSERTS

print

The fig-swirl: melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the sausepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, untill you have a chunky-jummy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, granulated sugar untill sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set the mixture in the fridge and wait untill the fig mixture is cold.

Using an icecream machine, pour liquids into the frozen bassin and procces according to manufacture instructions, i.e. , let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascrepone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour simi-forzen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • ☐ 1 1/2 cups milk
  • ☐ 1/2 cup mascrepone
  • ☐ 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • ☐1 lb black mission figs
  • ☐ 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • ☐ 2-4 tbsp water
  • ☐ 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • ☑ 1/3 granulated sugar
  • ☑ 2 egg yolks
  • ☐ 1 lemon juice
  • ☐2tbsp butter
  • ☐1 cup honey roasted pecans, roghly chopped
